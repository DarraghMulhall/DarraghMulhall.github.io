Big Five Personality Twitter Research

Hello. My name is Darragh Mulhall. I am a 4th year computer scientist in University College Dublin. For my final year project, I am researching the use of social media for predicting the personality type of a user. The personality model being used is the Big Five (See here).

I have made this web application to collect publicly available data from the accounts of Twitter users as well as their actual personality trait scores as determined by the test provided, which will both be used in conjunction for analysis. I would be grateful if you could provide me access to your Twitter data and complete the Big Five test provided (only 20 questions). The Twitter data is actually publicly accessible to anyone who knows your username, so it would be great if you could provide me this. The data extracted includes the posts you have made, your replies and retweets, your numbers of followers and the number of users you follow, the hashtags you use, as well as the number of posts you have favourited and the number of favourites people have given you.

All data collected will be used exclusively for research purposes. The data will not be made available to any other parties.

By providing your username you are giving me consent to use access your Twitter Data. Thank you!
